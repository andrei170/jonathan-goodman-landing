FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to be a certified trainer?

Yes, the Academy is built for currently certified personal trainers. If you're not yet certified, we recommend completing your fundamentals certification first.

Is this a certification or a course?

Both. OTA awards certification on completion. The bulk of the curriculum is business education — the certification is the wrapper around the income-producing skills.

How long until I have my first online client?

Most students land their first paying online client within 30-60 days of starting. First 10 clients usually within 90-120 days.

Can I keep my in-person clients?

Yes. Most students run hybrid for the first 6-12 months while building online. Once online income exceeds in-person, many drop the gym work.

Who is this NOT for?

Trainers expecting passive income. Online training is active work — the leverage comes from systems, not from doing nothing.
